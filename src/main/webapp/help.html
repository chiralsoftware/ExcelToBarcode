Help! What is this?

You have a spreadsheet and you need to generate labels, with barcode. ExcelToBarcode.com takes your spreadsheet and turns every row into a label which you can then print.

How do I contact you?

Email: info@chiralsoftware.com . We're happy to discuss improvements and any licensing opportunities.

What do you do with my data?

We do not store your data. We generate the labels but make no other use of the data. This site uses strong encryption for security so your data are safe.

How do I print the barcodes?

ExcelToBarcode.com will generate a PDF file output. It will be compatible with one of the standard label paper formats. You can buy these label papers at any office supply location, or on Amazon.

Load the label sheets into your printer, print, and you have labels.

How do I control the format of the labels?

ExcelToBarcode.com will take the format from the original spreadsheet. Change the cell alignment (left, right, or center), the font size, font, or add bold or italic. ExcelToBarcode.com will pick these up and use them.

The text in my label is too small

Increase the font size within the Excel spreadsheet.

The text is too big and the lines are wrapping

Decrease the font size within the Excel spreadsheet.
